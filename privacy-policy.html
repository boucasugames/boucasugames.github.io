Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartNote AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Controller

SmartNote AI is operated by Bouhisse.M We are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Audio Content

  • Voice recordings created within the app
  • Imported audio or video files
  • YouTube links submitted for transcription

2.2 AI Chat Data

  • Text messages you enter into the AI assistant

2.3 Device & Usage Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Feature usage statistics

2.4 Subscription Data

  • Subscription status managed via Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Subscription analytics processed via RevenueCat

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide transcription services
  • To generate AI chat responses
  • To manage subscriptions
  • To improve app performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. AI Processing and Third-Party Providers

Audio recordings and chat messages are transmitted securely to third-party AI providers solely to provide the requested service.
  • Deepgram: Processes audio for speech-to-text transcription.
  • Groq: Processes chat messages using large language models.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status.

These providers may process data on servers located in the United States.

5. Data Retention

  • Audio files are not permanently stored by us after transcription is completed.
  • Transcription results are stored locally on your device unless you delete them.
  • Chat messages may be temporarily processed but are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Subscription and analytics data may be retained as required for billing and legal compliance.

6. Data Deletion

You may delete your transcriptions at any time within the app.

You may request full data deletion by contacting us via email. We will process deletion requests in accordance with applicable laws.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our AI service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to such transfers.

8. Data Security

  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data.
  • Access to data is limited to authorized service providers.

9. Advertising & Analytics

We may use third-party analytics and advertising services such as AppsFlyer, Google Ads, Meta, and TikTok. Advertising identifiers are collected only with user consent.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR or CCPA including:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of personal data
  • Withdrawal of consent

11. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
