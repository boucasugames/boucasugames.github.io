Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Speech To Text ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Audio Data

  • Voice Recordings: We process audio recordings you create within the app for transcription purposes
  • Imported Audio/Video Files: We process audio and video files you import for transcription
  • YouTube Content: We process audio from YouTube links you provide for transcription

1.2 User Data

  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you use the app, including transcription counts and session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Subscription Data: Information related to your premium subscription through RevenueCat

1.3 Technical Data

  • Error Logs: Technical information to help us improve app performance
  • Network Information: Connection status for service availability

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Transcription Services: Converting your audio content to text using AI services
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  • User Support: Providing technical assistance and customer support

2.2 Premium Features

  • Subscription Management: Processing premium subscriptions through RevenueCat
  • Usage Tracking: Monitoring usage limits for free and premium users

3. Data Processing and Storage

Important: Your audio recordings are processed for transcription and are not permanently stored on our servers unless explicitly saved by you within the app.

3.1 Audio Processing

  • Audio files are temporarily processed through secure AI transcription services
  • Transcribed text is stored locally on your device and optionally on our secure servers
  • Original audio files are deleted from our servers after processing

3.2 Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Server data is stored with encryption at rest
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 AI Transcription Services

  • AssemblyAI: For advanced audio transcription features

4.2 Analytics and Subscriptions

  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions and user entitlements

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services necessary for app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Retention

  • Transcriptions: Stored until you delete them from the app
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Audio Files: Temporarily processed and immediately deleted after transcription
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Control

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: Delete your transcriptions and account data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Export: Export your transcriptions from the app

7.2 App Permissions

  • Microphone: Required for audio recording (can be disabled in device settings)
  • File Access: For importing audio/video files (optional)
  • Network: For transcription services and app functionality

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (which we do not engage in).
